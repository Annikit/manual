Troubleshooting

MagicTune may not work in case monitor is replaced or driver of graphic card is updated while MagicTune is operating. If so, please restart the system.


Check when MagicTune does not function properly.

* MagicTune feature is found only on PC (VGA) with Window OS that supports Plug and   Play.

* To check whether your PC is available for MagicTune feature, follow the
  steps below (When Windows is XP);

Control Panel -> Performance and Maintenance -> System -> Hardware -> Device Manager -> Monitors -> After deleting Plug and Play monitor, find 'Plug and Play monitor' by searching new Hardware.
